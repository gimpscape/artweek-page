Gimpscape Artweek

Online workshops and sharing sessions on current topics for all FOSS designer enthusiasts. The event was organized by the Indonesian Gimpscape Community, the largest FOSS Design Community in Indonesia. Everyone is welcome to join Gimpscape Artweek, including you!

Call for Paper

We open up opportunities for anyone working in the world of design and digital art (specifically those who use open source) to get involved in this event. For this reason, we open registration papers for prospective presenters.

Participant Registration

Gimpscape Artweek is an open activity that can be followed by anyone. However, to facilitate the management of activities, we require that potential participants register themselves. Registration will be open until the day of the event.

Schedule

Artweek Gimpscape will be held on 27-28 June 2020 starting at 09.00 WIB s.d. at 4:30 pm (GMT+7). This activity will be carried out online, information about classes, list of presenters, as well as the full schedule will be informed immediately.
