How to Be Participant?

Gimpscape Artweek is an open activity that can be followed by anyone. However, to facilitate the management of activities, we require that potential participants register themselves. GimpScape Artweek will be held for two consecutive days, namely on the 27-28 June 2020. Participant registration will be opened on 6-26 June 2020.

One of the requirements for participating in this activity is that potential participants will be asked to participate in donating with a minimum nominal value of IDR 20,000 (for Indonesian) and $10 (for foreign participant). The donations collected will be forwarded to open source application developers such as Inkscape, Gimp, Krita, or others. A report on the development of these donations will be available on the  Donation Report page.

If you have difficulty transferring to the account above, please contact us. Make sure the e-mail address is active, because access to the Artweek class will later be sent to the e-mail address used to register no later than D-1 prior to the event. We invite you if you want to try a room test beforehand at the H-1 event to get used to the platform that we use.

Thank you for participating in the Gimpscape Artweek 2020! See you in the classroom!
